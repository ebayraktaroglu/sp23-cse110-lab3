This document is a preserved record of our company's previous ill-advised project to create a time machine. This document preserves the meeting minutes of the endeavor of Ms. Stevenson and her team to create a functioning time machine that can go to the past and future. This document is a warning against this path. It was recorded by one of the members in the group, Tessa Smith, as the others attempted to destroy all evidence of this project. A majority of the data are missing, but a some are intact. Read with caution.
